About

  • February, 2000

    The Birth of The Company

    Blair Smart the founder of the company decided create a company that did residencial and commerical rennovations. Their primary focas was insurance rennovation.

  • 2003

    Opportunities Arise

    Blair had been working in the industry doing constuction for many of years and had a reputation with the hospitality industy. He had lots of success working with companys like TPG and Marriot.

  • 2008

    Transition Only Hospitality

    During the recession of 2008 Blair was forced to downsize and turn away to from the residencial aspect part of his company and focus solely on hospitality. Because of his connections he was able to keep the company afloat

  • Present Day

    Rebirth

    After recovering from the ressesion Blair has lead the company towards bigger and better things. The company purchased a building and was able to grow and continues to find success in working hard to achieve the best service as possible.
